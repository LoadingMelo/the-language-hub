## Introduction

Pronouns are a big part of what you will do in French

### Personal Pronouns

| Pronoun | Translation         | Pronunciation |
| ------- | ------------------- | ------------- |
| je      | I                   | zhe           |
| tu      | You (informal)      | to            |
| il      | He                  | ill           |
| elle    | She                 | el            |
| nous    | We                  | nu            |
| vous    | You (formal/plural) | vhu           |
| ils     | They (masculine)    | ill           |
| elles   | They (feminine)     | el            |
